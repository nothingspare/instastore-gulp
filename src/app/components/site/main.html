isOpen.us is a selling app that lets you build and operate an e-commerce business from your phone.

You can take pictures and list items from your phone or computer as well as batch import pics from Instagram.

Post items to Facebook, Instagram and Pinterest and when your items sell you get text messages with a link to free shipping labels.

isOpen.us app gives you USPS flat rate boxes so there’s no weighing items, just leave the boxes for your mailman to pick up.

Once item are accepted by the buyer you get paid directly to your debit card via a 100% secure service.

Wouldn’t it be great if every Mom & Pop business had an online store app that was just as easy to find.

Set up

It’s simple to set up your online store app. Verify your phone number, location and upload your stores background image.

Item Hosting

It’s free to list items for sale. You can do it from your phone or import pics from Instagram and add a price.

Shipping

When your item sells print free pre-addressed shipping labels and send them in USPS boxes the app provides.

Who we are

IsOpen is run by a Mom & Pop - David and Tona and a small team of developers, digital marketers and advertisers. We are based in Mt Washington near Los Angeles’s Downtown and have three kids.

We are really into supporting small independent sellers and businesses and want to do our part to make the internet a place where by doing things better small sellers can compete with huge online stores and trading giants.

We love to connect with our store owners and get feedback on the service and hear any other ways we can help promote their businesses.

If we are all connected, share ideas and are dedicated to growing our businesses together we will all be stronger for it. Please drop us and email and we really hope you love operating your store on isOpen.
