Congratulations new store owner! Let's get you set up.


Firstly:


Enter your stores details and background image via
         user profile.

Secondly:


Stock your store. Upload item photos from Instagram
         in the user profile or via the add item button
       and your phone's photo library. Add prices in the edit item tab.

Thirdly:


Invite your friends and customers. Link your social accounts and paste your store and item page urls to your blogs.

Then answer messages as they come in, you can edit item details at anytime and promote your items from the tabs on your item pages.

Once an item sells you'll get free shipping labels and instructions on which Free Flat Rate USPS Priority Mail box to stick it on. There's no weighing, no need to go to the post office and payments are automaticly paid to the debit card you link to your store.

Forthly:


Throw a party. Once your stores set up, put some special offers on your items and use the included promote tools to invite everyone to your sales event.

Best of luck from the isOpen team.
